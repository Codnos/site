Our Services

  • Bespoke solution development

    Whether you have a well thought project or just an idea that you want to test, we're here to help. Our team can help you with all the stages of software delivery. The code that we produce follows best practices including SOLID, DDD, TDD, BDD, GoF Patterns, OOP, FP. It is clean, mercilessly refactored and very thoroughly tested. And it does what it says on the tin.

  • Transforming existing products

    Sometimes you would love to be able to start over and just do it "this time properly". But you cannot always afford that. Is your technology stack outdated? Performance below your expectations? Is it not able to scale? Are you afraid to touch it because it may break? Do not despair. Our team can help you bring a new life into your neglected product.

  • Product design and prototyping

    Have you got an idea that doesn't let you sleep at night? Do you think it would revolutionize your industry? But is it really something that would work? Is it even feasible? Or maybe you need some help in transforming a business idea into a product. We will be delighted to offer you our services.

  • Code and delivery advice

    Is your code of high quality? Is it future proof? Is your software delivery team doing a good job? Are they following the best practices? Is there something they could do better? Can your software support your organisation well when it grows? We can help you answer all of those questions.
