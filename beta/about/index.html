story

We are Codnos and we've been helping businesses realize their full potential since 2016. We believe in doing things the right way and using technological and methodology advances to create software that delivers on its promise.

Over the years of working for many organisations we've seen a lot of badly delivered software projects. Some of them were never delivered, leading to wasting hundreds of thousands of pounds. We knew that it is possible to create software that works well for its users without overpaying for it as we've been doing this for years. That's why we started Codnos, the company that has knowledge from personal experience of efficient code delivery.

We love creating software that does great things for our clients but we love even more seeing how working together helps them achieve their goals quicker, more efficiently, and get their organisation to the next level.

mission

Our passion of turning pure ideas into something that actually works and provides value is what drives us. We want to help our customers understand their needs, create viable plan, validate it and turn it into a long term, strategic and future-proof solution. A lot of companies try to do that. What makes us different is our approach to your business. Not only we work with you to understand and deliver the software that you want us to help with. We become experts in your domain who can help you grow your business much further than software itself would.

values

  • pragmatism

    We work according to the best practices which help us deliver software of the highest quality on agreed schedule. This does require effort and time to be put in to reap all the benefits in the future. Sometimes the reality is that the delivery has to happen here and now or else the business looses the opportunity. In situations like that we remain pragmatic and make sure that delivery happens with enough quality to satisfy the current need. Accrued technical debt is dealt with immediately after the delivery.

  • reliability

    Our priority is making sure that our customers are satisfied with the software that they use. This requires a lot of dedication and focus on what is most important for them. A lot of hard work goes into meeting the current business needs and ensuring that the software remains ready for the future changes. Our people have years of experience of consistently delivering the highest quality of service. Even if this sometimes requires picking up the phone in the middle of the night and sorting out the issue. Codnos Ltd, at your service!

  • lean philosophy

    Years of experience have lead us to believe that one of the worst enemies of any sustainable business is waste. It can creep into any organisation, big or small. It can affect any aspect of it and cause "death by a thousand cuts". Our way of working reflects our desire to avoid waste wherever possible. From Just-In-Time analysis through avoiding defects by obsessively testing the product to intimate knowledge of our development tools we strive for elimination of all that is unnecessary.
