services

We offer a wide range of services for our customers. We streamline your business processes and help your business achieve its strategic goals. Please read below high level description of how we can help you.

  • bespoke solution development

    Whether you have a well thought over project or just an idea that you want to test, we're here to help. Our team can help you at all stages of software delivery. We follow the best practices including SOLID, DDD, TDD, BDD, GoF Patterns, OOP, FP. The solution is easy to maintain and thoroughly tested. And it does what it says on the tin.

  • transforming existing products

    Sometimes you would love to be able to start over and "do it properly, this time". But you cannot always afford that and have to live with what you have. At least most of it. Is your technology stack outdated? Performance below your expectations? Is it not able to scale? Are you afraid to touch it because it may break? Do not despair. Our team can help you bring a new life into your neglected product.

  • design and prototyping

    Have you got an idea that doesn't let you sleep at night? Do you think it would revolutionize your industry? But is it really something that would work? Is it even feasible? Or maybe you need some help in transforming a business idea into a product. We will be delighted to offer you our services.

  • code and delivery consulting

    Is your code of high quality? Is it future proof? Is your software delivery team doing a good job? Are they following the best practices? Is there something they could do better? Can your software support your organisation well when it grows? We can help you answer all of those questions.

expertise

We are focused on helping your business thrive, whatever technologies are necessary to achieve this goal. But if you're interest to know a bit more of what we've used in the past please read below...

  • jvm languages

    If the code is running on JVM then we should be the people working on it. We've got intimate knowledge of the JVM and have amassed years of experience with languages like: Java, Scala, Kotlin, Groovy.

  • js ecosystem

    We have built great UIs on ReactJS and VueJS with both ECMAScript and TypeScript. If you need any work to be done in this area we can surely help!

  • full stack development

    Our team have got lots of experience building all types of software covering all layers of the application. We can help you create beautiful and intuitive User Interfaces with scalable and easy to extend backend services.

  • test automation

    We can help you with creating testing strategy and automating your test suite for all levels and types of testing, e.g. Unit, Integration, System, Acceptance, UAT, Regression and Performance.

  • deployment automation

    Are you wondering if your releases could be less problematic and much shorter? You could avoid all those unnecessarily long hours going step by step through a badly written document and just have one-click-deployment. We've automated deployments of many systems using tools like Ansible and Puppet.

  • continuous delivery

    Why not go a step further in your automation and always be ready to ship your software? By including all the best practices and processes you can get your software into a state where only minutes separate you from writing code to deploying it into production.

  • micro-service architectures

    New trends in software creation allow better modularisation and independent, safe deployments of logical units of functionality. They also allow scaling your system and isolating it from the failures of other components. If you're looking to transform your application from monolithic architecture to micro-services, we're the people who can get you there.

  • reactive functional programming

    With Moore's Law reaching its limits we can no longer rely on increasing the power of CPUs to make our applications run faster or be able to handle more users. We have to instead become smarter about how we write the code to use the parallel processing power of modern multi-core processors. Using immutable data structures, pure functions, lock-free algorithms and reactive streaming with back-pressure allows maximizing the benefits of modern day parallel computing. We will help you benefit from the future of high performance computing.

  • distributed computing

    Some problems are easy and can be solved in milliseconds on one machine, some need hours on a single machine. But when split into small, independent units of work and run on a cluster of thousands of machines they can deliver the result in seconds. If you believe your application has reached its limits but your business needs it to perform much better to be able to scale we can make it happen!
